A visualization of the U.S. affordability crisis.

Created by Matthew Pearson

Wages vs Housing Prices

This chart compares median household income (blue line) to median home sale prices (red line) over time.

Notice how since the 1980s, housing prices have risen much faster than wages, widening the affordability gap.

This trend has made homeownership increasingly difficult for many Americans.

Unemployment Rates for College Graduates

This chart shows unemployment rates for college educated individuals ages 20-24 (blue line) compared to the overall rate of unemployment, nationwide (red line).

The unemployment rate of college students and recent graduates fluctuates greatly by season, but historically oscillating around the overall rate of unemployment.

However, since the COVID-19 pandemic, the unemployment rate for college students and recent graduate age 20-24 has consistently stayed above the overall rate of unemployment.
